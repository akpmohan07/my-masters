# Practicum — Subject Context

> **Module:** Practicum (Research Project) | **Programme:** MSc Computing | **Student:** Mohankumar Muthusamy

## Overview

The Practicum is Mohankumar's **research project** focused on **LLM (Large Language Model) Security — Adversarial Attacks and Defence Mechanisms**. This folder contains the Zotero reference library used to manage research papers and web sources for the literature review.

## Research Focus

### Primary Topic: LLM Security & Adversarial Attacks
Investigating how large language models can be attacked (jailbreaking, prompt injection, adversarial inputs, model extraction) and what defence frameworks exist to mitigate these threats.

### Secondary Topic: Gen Z Consumer Behaviour & Digital Marketing
Research into how Gen Z interacts with social media, brand engagement patterns, and digital marketing effectiveness — likely a supporting or comparative research angle.
